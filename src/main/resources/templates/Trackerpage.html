Goal-Setting Tips

Emergency Fund First

Build 3–6 months of expenses before any other goal. It protects all your other plans.

Automate Contributions

Set up automatic transfers on payday. What you don't see, you don't spend.

Set a Deadline

Goals with specific dates are 3× more likely to be achieved. Make it concrete.

Prioritize Wisely

Focus on 1–3 goals at a time. Spreading too thin slows all progress equally.
